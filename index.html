Cultivating underserved and underrepresented high school students to succeed academically and professionally through innovative solutions.


Until we get equality in education, we won't have an equal society.

Sonia Sotomayor



Rising is a nonprofit start-up that acts based on the belief that socioeconomic inequality in society is absolutely, fundamentally linked to inequality in access to education.






Bridging the Gaps





The
Achievement Divide

Failure in high school is the result of various factors such as differential learning growth and summer learning loss.

Rising supports students of color and other learners through initiatives that are accommodating, non-discriminatory, and enriching.




The
Digital Divide

There are deep consequences of the disparity that results from unequal exposure to technology at homes and schools.

Rising strives to create an inclusive digital environment for high school students so that they are well-equipped for the demands of the 21st century.




The
Networking Divide

Where you grow up, where you go to school, and where you work can give you an advantage in gaining access to opportunity.

Rising's vision is to create economic opportunity for underrepresented high school students through technology and programs.








There is no better way for college-bound students to get a feel for the future than participating in pre-college programs.

High school students can build relationships, explore career options, and discover more about themselves through these opportunities.








Our goal is to build a movement to connect every young person to the kind of meaningful relationships that provide us all with networks of support and opportunity.

Transforming the lives of the next generation in communities of the highest need is possible with your help.
