RISING* is an education nonprofit that aims to cultivate the minds of learners by providing the right conditions for them to grow personally and professionally.

In order to achieve equality and accessibility in education, we help equip schools with the learning tools and skills needed to overcome the unique barriers faced by students - individually and by populations.

Networking Program

The "Networking Program" is a program geared towards mentoring low-income students and closing the network gap. As participants create a social impact project, they are also connected with mentors and coaches.
