Share Your Story
to
Empower Minority Youth



We all have a story to tell encompassing both difficulties and successes.

When we tell our stories, we show others that we are, in fact, more like them than they may imagine.

Rising would like to celebrate the diversity and commonality of human experience across disciplines through its first magazine issue.







Success in our modern economy demands a wide set of skills, including digital literacy, leadership, and creativity just to name a few. High school students need access to opportunities to develop these highly adaptable skills early so that they are better prepared to take on challenges and opportunities.







Issue No. 1



Our magazine aims to empower young voices through a showcasing of diverse people who have overcome challenges in their studies and/or careers. We want each story to be inspirational and every voice authentic.

We are working on having the magazine available digitally and in print. For the printed magazines, they will be distributed to libraries and high schools.








There is power in the vulnerability within each of our stories to inspire others. Our mistakes, failures, problems, and ultimate successes can be insightful to high school students who are experiencing or have yet to experience those things. As an advocate for diversity and inclusion, you can elevate the voices of others like you and other underrepresented people to greater visibility.







Magazines have an array of content and media. Thus, in order to create and share our first digital magazine, we are looking for bright, creative talent to be part of our team.
