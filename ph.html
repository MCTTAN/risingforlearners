Cultivating learners to flourish - regardless of background, circumstances, and surroundings.






Education is an essential human right. But some countries struggle to provide it.

Deep inequalities of wealth, gender, ethnicity, language, location and disabilities continue to hold back academic progress.

Too many learners still leave education with skills that are not relevant for their personal fulfillment, including in the world of work.

We are working to ensure that education is not only accessible for all Filipinos, but also has a meaningful impact for Philippine society.
