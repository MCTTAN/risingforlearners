About



Mission


Our mission is to provide the right conditions for high school students to thrive personally and professionally. We strive to realize every student's potential by equipping them with the tools and skills needed to overcome the unique socioeconomic barriers faced - individually and by populations.





Far from being the meritocracy that many people believe it to be, the education sector has become increasingly elitist in a society that is permeated with social inequalities, as various gaps continue to widen.

For many high school students who face socioeconomic or health challenges, receiving a quality education has the potential to change the trajectory of their lives. Yet, education has become more of a competition that favors those who have a headstart - with money, power, and influence.


Volunteers



Our volunteers are dedicated to tackling the perplexing issues that high school students face both individually and by population groups. They are catalysts for change in not only education, but also in raising the standards of living and improving the quality of lives.
